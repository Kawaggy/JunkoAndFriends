Adds various Vanities. Buy them from the Clothier or fight random enemies.
For Gura Gawr vanity kill Sharks or buy from the Pirate Post-Moonlord.
For Mori Calliope vanity kill Reapers or buy from the Clothier post Solar-Eclipse.
For Usada Pekora vanity kill bunnies or buy from the Clothier after 150 bunny kills.
Craft Remilia's wings or Flandre's wings in Hardmode.
